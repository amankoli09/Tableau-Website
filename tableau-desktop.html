Tableau Desktop

Explore, model, and visualize data anytime — even offline. Tableau Desktop empowers analysts to turn data into impactful insights with speed and confidence.

Self-service data modeling and analytics

Discover insights faster with powerful features that offer flexibility and control. From multi-fact relationships to custom themes, viz extensions, and enhanced accessibility, Tableau Desktop puts you in charge.

Connect to data from anywhere, combine and clean with clicks, and transform raw data into confident decisions.

Tableau Desktop and Tableau Prep are available via Creator licenses. Ask about Tableau+ to see how you can drive speed to insight with trusted AI.

Key Features

Simplify unlimited data exploration

From data preparation to consumption, Tableau Desktop gives you everything you need to deliver real-time insights. Ask new questions, spot trends, identify opportunities, and make data-informed decisions with confidence.

Drive smarter decisions with AI

Expedite business results with intelligent insights you can trust. Transparent agentic capabilities help democratize what-if scenario planning, guided model building, and AI-powered predictions.

Integrate and visualize live results from R, Python, MATLAB, and other extensions to scale advanced analytics across teams.

Collaborate and share

Tableau provides a governed, flexible environment for teams to collaborate on trusted data. Integrations with Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams, Slack, and more help break down silos.

Augment productivity with AI- and ML-powered experiences that guide users to answers in ways they understand.
