Tableau Server

Unleash the power of the Tableau platform with unparalleled flexibility. Deploy on-premises, public, or private to meet your compliance and regulatory needs while maintaining full control of your analytics.

Deploy visual, self-service analytics with unmatched control and flexibility.

Tableau Server is a self-managed, enterprise-grade analytics solution that empowers faster data-driven decisions. It supports every stage of analysis — from data preparation to exploration and visualization.

Integrate Tableau Server with your existing systems and scale analytics securely across your organization.

Get a Tableau Creator License

Get Tableau Server along with Tableau Desktop and Tableau Prep Builder as part of Tableau Creator.

Get to insights faster with a trusted AI assistant.

Simplify your data journey and accelerate analysis with Tableau Agent in Tableau Server. Use natural language to move seamlessly from preparation to visualization and exploration.

Integrate unparalleled analytics into your AI ecosystem.

Integrate Tableau into your AI agents and applications using ready-to-use tools. Build governed and secure AI experiences that connect directly to your Tableau data.

Match your business needs with simple cost management and powerful access controls.

Tableau Server offers role-based and core-based licensing options. Role-based licensing keeps costs predictable, while core-based licensing enables wide sharing without extra fees.

Strong access controls ensure users see the right data while maintaining enterprise-grade governance.
