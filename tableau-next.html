Tableau Next

Agentic analytics that delivers personalized, contextual, and actionable insights everywhere work happens.

The future of analytics has arrived. Meet Tableau Next.

Tableau Next is a flexible, API-first analytics experience that integrates the #1 analytics platform with Agentforce, the world’s first digital labor platform. Built on a composable architecture with a unified data layer and trusted semantics, it delivers personalized and actionable insights to every user.
